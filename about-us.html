About Us

Aditya Patil

Aditya Patil is the heart and soul behind Maharashtrian Wedding — a passionate storyteller who believes every Maharashtrian wedding is a beautiful tapestry of traditions, emotions, and timeless memories. With years of experience in photography and videography, Aditya has mastered the art of capturing the vibrant rituals, heartfelt moments, and intricate details that make each wedding unique.
But Aditya’s work goes far beyond the lens. As a dedicated wedding partner, he ensures every couple’s journey is seamless, offering not just world-class photography but also personalized guidance — from helping you find the perfect traditional attire and decor to connecting you with trusted makeup artists, mehndi experts, and vendors. His deep cultural understanding, creative eye, and commitment to excellence make him a trusted name for couples who want their Maharashtrian wedding captured with authenticity and soul.
With Aditya Patil and Maharashtrian Wedding by your side, your wedding isn’t just documented — it’s celebrated, cherished, and preserved for generations to come.
